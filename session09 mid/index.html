About aespa

Group Introduction

aespa is a South Korean girl group formed by SM Entertainment and debuted on November 17, 2020. The group consists of Karina, Giselle, Winter and Ningning.

They are known for pioneering the metaverse concept and the hyper-pop sound in K-pop, blending virtual avatars with strong visual storytelling.

Meaning of the Name

The name aespa combines “avatar” + “experience” (Avatar × Experience) and the word “aspect,” representing the meeting of two sides — reality and virtual identity — to explore a new digital world.
