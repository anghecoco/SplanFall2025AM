Splatoon 3 is a third-person action game where teams compete by covering the map in ink. Its identity is built on bold color, street-style visuals, and fast match pacing.

Gameplay modes (quick look)

Competitive matches with more intense objectives and faster decision-making.

Salmon Run (Co-op)

Team up, complete waves, and collect eggs in a hectic, teamwork-focused mode.
