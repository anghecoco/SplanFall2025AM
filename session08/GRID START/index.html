Hydnellum caeruleum

Exploring the blue mushroom

About the Species

Hydnellum caeruleum, also known as the blue tooth fungus, is a unique mushroom species found in pine forests of North America and Europe. It is recognized for its striking blue cap and spiny underside.

Although it looks beautiful, this mushroom is not edible. It plays an important role in forest ecosystems by forming symbiotic relationships with tree roots.
